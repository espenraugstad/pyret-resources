Background

According to the official website, Pyret "is a programming language designed to serve as an outstanding choice for programming education while exploring the confluence of scripting and functional programming."

Being a programming language for educational use, it was the language of choice for introduction to programming in the course Introduction to co-creation in Information Systems at the University of Agder in the fall of 2023. The resources on this page are primarily related to the work done in this course. They are therefore not intended to be exhaustive.

The repo
